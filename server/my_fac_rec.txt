Title: Spaghetti Aglio e Olio
Ingredients: spaghetti, olive oil, garlic, red chili flakes, parsley, salt
Instructions: 
  Boil the spaghetti in salted water. 
  Meanwhile, sauté garlic and chili flakes in olive oil. 
  Drain the pasta and combine with the garlic oil mixture. 
  Top with chopped parsley.
Taste: savory
Cuisine: Italian
PrepTime: 20
Reviews: 25

Title: Chicken Curry
Ingredients: chicken, onion, garlic, ginger, curry powder, coconut milk, salt, oil
Instructions:
  Sauté onion, garlic, and ginger in oil. 
  Add chicken and curry powder, cook until chicken is sealed. 
  Pour in coconut milk and simmer until chicken is cooked through.
Taste: spicy
Cuisine: Indian
PrepTime: 30
Reviews: 40
